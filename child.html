Child Safety Standards

Palki Matrimony – Marriage App

Our Commitment to Child Safety

Palki Matrimony is committed to protecting children from sexual abuse and exploitation. We strictly prohibit any form of child sexual abuse material (CSAM) and take strong action against users who violate our policies.

Prohibited Content

  • Any form of child sexual abuse or exploitation
  • Sharing, storing, or distributing CSAM
  • Grooming or inappropriate interaction with minors
  • Content that sexualizes or endangers children

Reporting Child Safety Concerns

Users can report child safety concerns directly within the Palki Matrimony app. All reports are reviewed promptly, and appropriate action is taken, including account suspension and reporting to authorities when required.

Legal Compliance

Palki Matrimony complies with all applicable local, regional, and international child safety laws. We cooperate fully with law enforcement agencies and report confirmed CSAM cases to relevant national and regional authorities.
